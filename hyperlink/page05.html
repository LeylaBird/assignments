Rules are very important when starting the school year with a new roommate. These are your baseline for the others to know how much they can tolerate and not flip out because something wasn't brought up and they go crazy. Some things that could be put down areâ€¦
  • Quiet Hours (what does being quiet look like?)
  • Guest policies (what gender, how long?)
  • Cleaning schedule (what is clean to you?)
  • Notify if you change the temperature (what temperatures are reasonable?)
  • Respecting personal property (what the others can/cannot touch)
It is essential that you have a solid base in the beginning so you don't kill your roommate for torching you.
