When starting out with your roommate you need a stable way of communication. There are always ways you can talk in person, but sometimes you need to communicate with them and you don't know where they are. Some ways of communication are…
  • Microsoft Teams

Some other things to consider is the cleaning process of your living space. Some ways that some roommates do is having a rotating cleaning chart, having a designated space to keep clean, or cleaning up after yourself. These ways could cause some chaos so you need to be able to discuss and keep in mind “what's the definition of clean to you” because everyone has different views and if one roommate thinks clean is sparkling and no dust at all and the other views clean as things just needing to be where they are, may cause a issue. Miscommunication is one of the number one factors of roommates fighting and causing the other to strongly dislike the other. Before you even start to move on to your separate lives of living together just make sure you are all on the right page of your definitions and views.
